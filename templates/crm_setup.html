How CRM Sync Works

1

AI detects CRM-relevant updates

When processing your emails, Jottask identifies notes and updates for your CRM contacts.

2

You approve the update

CRM updates require your approval via email before being pushed.

3

Notes sync to your CRM automatically

Once approved, the note is added to the matching contact in your CRM. No manual entry needed.
